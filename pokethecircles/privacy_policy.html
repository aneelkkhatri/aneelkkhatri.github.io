Privacy Policy

Information Collected

We collect both Personal Information and Non-Personal Information.

We collect User's Display Name as Personal Information, and Score and Level as Non-Personal Information.

How Is Collected Information Used?

We use Personal and Non-Personal Information to create the scoreboard. The scoreboard, which contains entries of top 10 scorer, is visible to all players.

Where's Collected Information Stored

The information collected is stored at server database hosted by ServersFree

How the Collected Stored Information Secured and Disclosed

The information is secured by querying the server. The secured information is displayed in the game as scoreboard.

User's Control

Both Personal and Non-Personal Information is collected only if the user chooses to share it by clicking "Add to Global Scores" button. User can't undo it once the information (i.e. Score and Display Name) is shared.


By playing this game, you agree that you consent this privacy policy and any changes hereto in the future. You agree that we can make changes to this privacy policy at any time without any given notice at its discretion.
